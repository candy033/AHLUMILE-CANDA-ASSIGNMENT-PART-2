🍁 Feature Spotlight: Autumn Classes 2026 🍁

@hlumisi_imfundo 🍂📚 HLUMIS’IMFUNDO AUTUMN CLASSES 2026 📚🍂 We are excited to announce our 5-Day Autumn Classes Programme designed to help learners strengthen their understanding as we begin Term 2 curriculum preparation.

Tutoring

This foundation offers academic assistance in the following subjects Mathematics, Mathematical Literacy, Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, Business Studies, Accounting, we also tutor for primary school subjects such as Isixhosa, English, and Economic Management Science. The Hlumis' Imfundo Foundation also provides classes for those individuals who want to do a matric rewrite.

Mentorship

Career guidance and personal support from young professionals. These mentors serve as trusted role models, fostering personal growth, and confidence while providing support for career exploration and navigating the path to higher education.

Workshops

The foundation will provide workshops for Life skills, educate about different study techniques, and personal development for our students.

School Supplies

The foundation will provide stationery to those students in need, learning sources, and it will also provide sanitary pads to female learners who will be in need of them.
