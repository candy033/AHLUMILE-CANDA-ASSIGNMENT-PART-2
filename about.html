About Us

Hlumis’ Imfundo Foundation was established in 2018 to support learners in underserved communities. What began as a small volunteer group evolved into an organisation offering academic assistance, mentorship, and essential learning resources.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every learner has equal access to education and opportunities for success. This vision emphasizes equity,aiming to discouple a student's potential from their family income or background. The main vision that this foundation focuses on is dismantling systemic inequalities and foster transformative outcomes that allows students to break the cycle for poverty. This vision imagines a world where the brilliance of all students is celebrated and fully cultivated, recognizing that education is a powerful catalyst for social justice and upward mobility.

Our Target Audience

We support learners aged 10–21, parents, educators, volunteers, sponsors, and community partners.
